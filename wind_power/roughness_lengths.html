Reference for roughness length
Roughness length (m) Typical features
0.0002 open water, tidal flat, snow with fetch above 3 km
0.005 featureless land, ice
0.03 flat terrain with grass or very low vegetation, airport runway
0.10 cultivated area, low crops, obstacles of height H separated by at least 20 H
0.25 open landscape, scattered shelter belts, obstacles separated by approx. 15 H
0.5 landscape with bushes, young dense forest etc separated by approx. 10 H
1.0 open spaces comparable with H, eg mature forest, low-rise built-up area
> 2.0 irregular distribution of large elements, eg city centre, large forest with clearings
